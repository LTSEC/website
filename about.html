About LTSEC

LTSEC is the cybersecurity club at Louisiana Tech University. We're dedicated to the pursuit of cybersecurity knowledge through active learning, leadership, and mentorship.

Originally founded as the Cyber Security Defense Club (CSDC) in 2020 with the support of Mr. Michele Maasberg and Dr. Benjamin Drozdenko, LTSEC has grown to become the leading cybersecurity club at Louisiana Tech, competing in multiple competitions including the National Cyber Security, the Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition, and more.
